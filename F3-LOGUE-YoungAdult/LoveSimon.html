Love, Simon: Directed by Greg Berlanti and adapted from the novel Simon vs the homo sapiens agenda, this film is a sweet coming-of-age story of a teenager named Simon who debates on coming out to his family & friends as gay. The film has all the elements common to YA films with the pop soundtrack and the aesthetic backdrops and these film recommendations carry on the LGBTQIA+ thematic inferences of the movie along with the coming-of-age vibe.
