LOGUE

Howl's moving castle: Directed by Hayao Miyazaki, the premise of this whimsical animation is that an unconfident young woman is cursed with an old body by a spiteful witch, her only chance of breaking the spell lies with a self-indulgent yet insecure young wizard and his companions in his legged, walking castle. This logue captures the whimsical narrative elements of the film paired with the distinct design of Studio Ghibli and Miyazaki through it's recommendations
