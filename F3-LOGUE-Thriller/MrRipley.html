LOGUE

The Talented Mr.Ripley: Directed by Anthony Minghella, the film takes place in the late 1950s when Tom Ripley, a young underachiever, is sent to Italy to retrieve Dickie Greenleaf, a rich and spoiled millionaire playboy. The film takes on a morbid edge over time, quite like a descent into madness while being shot against the idyllic backdrop of Italy's provinces.
