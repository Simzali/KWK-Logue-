LOGUE

This contemporary romantic comedy, Crazy Rich Asians directed by Peter Chiarelli, Adele Lim, and Kevin Kwan, is adpated from a global bestseller where native New Yorker Rachel Chu to Singapore to meet her boyfriend's family.The film depicts the rollercoaster-esque journey Rachel faces as she attempts to navigate her boyfriend's "crazy rich" family and their dysfunctionality. This logue captures the dry humour elements the film is so popular for alongside the narrative focal point of a dysfunctional family.
